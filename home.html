About Prayana

Driving Innovation in Robotics

Prayana is a pioneering initiative by IEEE RAS Kerala Section, designed to bring together students, researchers, developers, and industry professionals passionate about robotics and automation.

Our mission is to create a collaborative platform that fosters innovation, knowledge sharing, and practical skill development in the field of robotics and automation technologies.

Through hands-on workshops, technical talks, competitions, and networking opportunities, Prayana aims to inspire the next generation of robotics engineers and enthusiasts to push the boundaries of what's possible.

Join us for this transformative journey where ideas come to life, connections are forged, and the future of robotics is shaped.
